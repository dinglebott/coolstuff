Solar System

Our Solar System primarily consists of a star and 8 planets. There are many other smaller objects orbiting it, such as the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter, and several dwarf planets like Pluto. The Sun is roughly 4.6 billion years old, and its planets and other satellites were formed from the surrounding cloud of gas and dust soon after its birth.
